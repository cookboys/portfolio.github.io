Make retro-like text

Illustrator {{< figure src="/images/photos/photo10_firstview.png" alt="">}}

Prepare the text

Prepare the text. The font selected "Raglan Punch UB" this time.

Make the font "outline".

Set the line to white (#ffffff) and the thickness to "10pt". Align the position of the line inside.

Fill the background to pink (# f16975).

To make it three-dimensional

Open the Appearance panel, add a line with dark blue (# 293454) and place it under the white line.

I will add an effect to the stroke of a dark blue line.

With the dark blue line stroke selected, from the Appearance panel, select New Effects → Deform Path → Transform.

"Horizontal direction: 1px" "Vertical direction: 1px" "Check for transformation of objects" "Check for transformation of patterns" "Copy: 20" and press OK.

Add a fill

From the Appearance panel, add the paint in light blue (# 39a4be).

Place the position under the dark blue line.

Add a shadow

From the Appearance panel, add a fill in black (# 000000).

From the Appearance panel, select New effect → Transform path → Transform.

"Horizontal direction: 1px" "Vertical direction: 1px" "Check for transformation of objects" "Check for transformation of patterns" "Copy: 50" Press OK.

Set to "Opacity: 20%".

Place the position under a light blue paint.

Add a pattern

I download the pattern from this site.

Create a fill with a pattern.

Change filling mode of fill to "soft light".

From the Appearance panel, position the paint on a light blue paint.

At the same time, since the shadow was formed, we placed the position of the letter somewhat above.

It is complete!!
