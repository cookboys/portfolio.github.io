Crop a person from a complex photograph

Photoshop {{< figure src="/images/photos/photo07_firstview_en.png" alt="">}}

Prepare a cropped image

I will prepare a picture of the woman whose ocean is reflected in the background.

Complex ocean background with hair dancing in the wind and rippling background.

It is difficult to cut out, but cut out only the person of this material.

Crop the image

Using "Quick Selection Tool", create selection area roughly along the person.

I use masks of "selection and mask" to mask the image roughly.

"Edge detection: 250 px" "Smart radius = put a check"

"Global adjustment" is fixed as follows. "Edge shift: 70%" "Output setting: Extract unnecessary color: Application degree 70%"

I use the "boundary line adjustment brush tool" to fill the hair and paint it in.

Correct the mask

Right-click on the layer panel → select "apply layer mask". Only image layers.

command (Win -> Ctrl) - Click to select a layer and create a selection.

We use "selection and mask" to create the mask again.

Set "Edge shift: -20%". The value sets the adaptation value by looking at the picture.

Since part of the body has been displayed, open the "Channel" panel and adjust the mask.

Using "Pen tool", create a path along the outline of the body. Create a selection by "Create selection".

Fill the body with white, paint around the body with black, and adjust the mask around the body.

Adjust the edge

Since "shift edge" is still weak, adjust the mask again by applying the edge.

Finally adjust finely

We will carefully remove garbage around the hair that is still left, using the "selection and mask" "brush tool".

Finally, "Create a new layer" → "Create a clipping mask". Change the drawing mode of the layer to "hue".

The color of hair is extracted with "eyedropper tool". Along the hair, paint the color with the brush tool and make the color of the hair more natural.

Composite

Combine with photos and it will be completed unless you feel strange.
