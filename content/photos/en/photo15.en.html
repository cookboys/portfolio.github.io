Logo combining various fonts and patterns

Illustrator {{< figure src="/images/photos/photo15_firstview.png" alt="">}}

Make a background

Look for thin wallpaper material on the material site, make it the background.

Make a letter

Use four kinds of fonts to make letters.

Cut out characters and synthesize

After making the character, execute "outline" and release the group.

Use "Knife tool" and cut characters appropriately.

After cutting the letters, we will arrange the parts randomly.

Fill with character pattern

After arranging the letters, open the pattern library from "Window" -> "Swatch library" -> "pattern" -> "basic".

Fill it appropriately with a pattern. Use "enlargement / reduction" "rotation" in some places, and change the pattern. When making a change, because it will deform graphics by default, check only "pattern deformation".

Change the drawing mode to "multiplication".

Change color

Use "Edit" → "Edit Color" → "Adjust Color Balance" to change the color of the pattern.

It is complete!!
