Using the "borderline adjustment brush tool", the hair is masked so as not to feel uncomfortable with a good feeling.

Fit the screen of the personal computer

Adjust the reflection of the screen

Along the screen of the computer, create a gradation of white to black.

Use the "blurring tool" to blur the edge of the right side.

Adjust reflection

After creating the gradation, change the layer to "Screen".

Then fine-tune the opacity of the layer.
