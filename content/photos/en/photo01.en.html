Prepare a cropped image

Crop the image

Using the "Pen tool" of Photoshop, we will trace around the image with a path.

Based on the path, create a selection and mask it.

Using the "borderline adjustment brush tool", the hair is masked so as not to feel uncomfortable with a good feeling.

Composite

Since it was possible to clip it, I will synthesize it with a nice touch! (Actually adjusts tone curve, color balance, etc.)
