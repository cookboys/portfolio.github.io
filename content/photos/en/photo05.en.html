Composite picture of the image inspired by the head

Photoshop {{< figure src="/images/photos/photo05_firstview_en.png" alt="">}}

Prepare a cropped image

Prepare an image of a woman holding a smartphone.

Crop the image

Using the "Pen tool" of Photoshop, we will trace around the image with a path.

Based on the path, create a selection and mask it.

Using the "borderline adjustment brush tool", the hair is masked so as not to feel uncomfortable with a good feeling.

Combine texture

Prepare a texture image and combine it into a photo.

Blur the texture tip

Use the selection area and mask to slightly blur the edges of the texture.

Select a texture in the round selection area and use Blur Border to blur the selection boundary.

Then apply a mask.

Composite

Because it was able to clip and synthesize, I will synthesize it with a good feeling! (Actually adjusts tone curve, color balance, etc.)
