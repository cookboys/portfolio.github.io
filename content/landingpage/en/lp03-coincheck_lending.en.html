CONCEPT

I designed the top page of Coincheck Lending (Rental virtual currency).

The concept is "the concept of a new virtual currency".

In the first view, I tried to place new images reminiscent of the feeling of the near future and remind me of new services different from other companies.

In addition, I put a bit of "pop" images, icons on the whole, I tried to design such that "bitterness" possessed by bit coins was dispelled.
