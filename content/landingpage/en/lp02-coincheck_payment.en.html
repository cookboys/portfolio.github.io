CONCEPT

I redesigned the top page of Coincheck Payment.

The concept was created by imagining "spread of bit coin settlement".

In the background circle and line, in the future bit-coin settlement spread all over the world, designing with the thought / wish that one of the world's basic currencies is a bit coin.

In addition, by expressing the merit of bit coin settlement with icon + character, we emphasized visually easy-to-understand and designed to eliminate further suspiciousness.
